# Kachamba Swim — landing page design

## Goal

Create a premium bilingual landing page for Mykyta Kachalaba that converts adult beginners in Kyiv into Instagram conversations. The page should make a first swimming lesson feel safe, personal, and aspirational.

## Audience and promise

The primary audience is adults who do not yet feel comfortable in water: they may be afraid of depth, struggle with breathing, tire quickly, or feel embarrassed about starting late.

The Ukrainian-first promise is: "Почніть плавати красиво." Supporting copy makes the outcome concrete: learn to feel safe, breathe calmly, and cover distance with confidence, without rushing or shame.

## Brand and visual direction

The chosen direction is **Deep Water**: an Apple-inspired boutique-luxury treatment, not a generic fitness-club design.

- Base: midnight-water blue / near-black background.
- Contrast: pearl-white editorial typography with muted ice-aqua accents.
- Atmosphere: sparse layout, generous whitespace, fine hairlines, translucent glass surfaces, restrained water-light movement.
- Imagery: selected genuine photos or clips from the supplied Instagram export; no stock imagery, fake testimonials, invented credentials, or unsupported numerical claims.
- Tone: controlled, calm, precise, supportive.

## Page structure

1. **Hero** — premium water visual, Ukrainian headline, concise beginner-focused promise, Instagram CTA, language switcher.
2. **Recognition** — four concise concerns the visitor may recognise: depth anxiety, breathing, fatigue, and not knowing how to begin.
3. **Method** — three stages: get comfortable in water, build breath and technique, develop confident distance / optional first start.
4. **Coach credibility** — Mykyta Kachalaba, swimming coach and HTF coach; concise personal positioning and direct link to @kachamba_swim.
5. **Real atmosphere** — selected gallery from supplied materials with an invitation to see current training and results on Instagram.
6. **Closing CTA** — Instagram conversation as the only conversion path.

## Language and interaction

- Ukrainian is the default language.
- A compact UA / EN control replaces all visible page text in place; it does not require separate routes or persistence.
- Every primary CTA opens `https://www.instagram.com/kachamba_swim/` in a new tab with an accessible label.
- The page respects reduced-motion preferences; water-light animation is decorative and disabled or simplified when appropriate.

## Implementation boundaries

- One responsive landing page; no forms, payments, accounts, server-side state, analytics dashboard, or content management system.
- Include semantic landmarks, keyboard-operable language controls, image alt text, and contrast-safe text.
- Add site-specific title, description, Open Graph metadata, and a bespoke social preview derived from the final visual system.

## Validation

Before publishing: successful production build, mobile and desktop visual check, verified language switching, verified Instagram links, accessible labels, and final metadata / social-preview checks.
